About Me

I am a Content Designer and Service Strategist with over 10 years of experience building bridges between complex systems and human needs. Recently relocated to Barcelona, I help local and global teams design services that are as intuitive as they are impactful.

Having worked across different markets, I understand the nuances of localisation and the importance of cultural empathy in digital products.

Obsession with Clarity

I thrive in complexity. My goal is always to reduce friction and cognitive load, making technology feel less like a hurdle and more like a conversation.
