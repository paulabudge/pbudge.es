About Me

Hi — I'm Paula. I'm a Content Designer and Service Strategist with a background in digital public services, UX research and conversational design. I work at the intersection of language, systems and human behaviour to make digital services kinder, clearer and more useful.

I'm currently based in Barcelona, working with organisations that care about the people they serve. I believe good content isn't decoration — it is the experience.
