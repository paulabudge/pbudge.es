Scope & Role

As Senior Content Lead, I directed a team of 10+ writers to provide the latest information about the COVID-19 pandemic in Peru, including state measures, health recommendations and the provision of digital government services.

I supervised the publication of orientation content, managed compliance with guidelines, and coordinated with government officials across different sectors for the provision of information and digital services.

Goals

  • Create a digital hub for all official information about the COVID-19 pandemic in Peru.
  • Mitigate misinformation about the pandemic with clear, accurate, timely content.
  • Create a digital access point for citizens in need of government assistance.

What I did

  • Information architecture for the platform.
  • Content strategy and editorial planning.
  • Review of UX design wireframes.
  • Proofreading, copyediting and compliance with guidelines.
