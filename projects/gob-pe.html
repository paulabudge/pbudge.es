Scope & Role

As Senior Content Lead at the Peruvian Presidency of the Council of Ministers (PROMSACE), I directed a team of 8+ writers creating orientation content about government procedures at local, regional and national levels.

I onboarded external communication specialists from government offices into the Gob.pe platform, ensuring compliance with content guidelines, service design principles and use of the proprietary CMS. I also coordinated with government officials across different sectors for the provision of information and digital services.

Goals

  • Provide citizens with easy access to institutional information, procedures and government services.
  • Improve the user experience and increase engagement on the platform.
  • Streamline content creation processes and maintain high-quality standards across multiple platforms.

What I did

  • Directed platform governance, establishing editorial and technical guidelines for content consistency and compliance.
  • Designed and executed a comprehensive G2C Content Strategy including instructional citizen guides, multi-sector impact stories and educational webinar scripts.
  • Acted as main liaison for partner interface management, coordinating platform onboarding for diverse government institutions.
  • Managed and mentored a team of junior and mid-level content specialists.
